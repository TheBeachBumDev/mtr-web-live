Approval Rules (Admin)

Rules decide who must approve and in what order. Each rule applies when the PO total falls in the min/max range (ZAR), and optionally when department and category match. Use step number 1 for the first approval, 2 for the next, and so on (single-step POs use step 1 only). Step role is the label shown on the PO (e.g. Finance, Site lead) — type any name; primary approver on the rule is who must approve.

“All departments” = rule applies everywhere. Pick one department to limit the rule to POs in that department only.
